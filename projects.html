PROJECT-1

PARALYSIS PATIENT HEALTH CARE SYSTEM USING IOT

The Paralysis Patient Healthcare System using IoT project aims to develop a comprehensive healthcare system that utilizes IoT (Internet of Things) technology to provide efficient and personalized care for patients with paralysis.This system integrates various IoT devices and sensors to monitor and assist patients in their daily activities, ensuring their well-being and improving their quality of life.

 Advantages

  1. Continuos monitoring of paralysis patient
  2. Secure and reliable Communication

 Applications

  1. Better access to health care
  2. Doctors can treat the patient from his cabin itself

PROJECT-2

WIRELESS TRANSMITTER AND RECEIVER USING RF MODULES

The RF Transmitter and Receiver project aims to establish a reliable wireless communication link using RF modules. It consists of an RF transmitter module that converts input data into RF signals for transmission, and an RF receiver module that receives and decodes these signals. By utilizing pre-designed transmitter and receiver circuits, the project simplifies the process of wireless communication over short to medium distances, offering flexibility and convenience in various applications such as home automation, wireless sensor networks, and remote control systems

  1. Transmitter and Receiver kept over a 20m distance
  2. Secure and Reliable communication
